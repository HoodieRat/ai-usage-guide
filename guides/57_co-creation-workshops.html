Co-Creation Workshops

Turn live co-creation sessions into shipping-ready AI workflows by scripting the experience, capturing outputs, and locking owners before the room clears.

Cross-links: Prep with Meetings & Agendas, capture outcomes in Knowledge Bases, and queue follow-ups via Automations & Reminders.
Data policy: Only use sanitized datasets. If unsure, defer the live prompt and log a follow-up action.

What you’ll learn

  • Scoping workshops that produce shippable prompts, workflows, or QA assets in one session.
  • Designing facilitation scripts that balance live prompting with synthesis.
  • Capturing owners, due dates, and storage locations before the room clears.

Prerequisites

  • Approved agenda, roles (facilitator, SME, scribe, decision-logger), and success criteria.
  • Sanitized datasets and pre-built prompt sandboxes.
  • Shared workspace (doc/board) with templates for notes, prompts, and decisions.

Planner → Actor → Reviewer walkthrough

Planner prompt

“Draft a 60‑minute workshop to produce a customer-support triage prompt with guardrails and examples. Include roles, timings, and outputs.”

Actor response

The assistant proposes segments (demo, build sprint, critique), supplies starter prompts, and lists artifacts to capture.

Reviewer check

Reviewer confirms policy coverage, approves datasets, and sets the acceptance checklist for outputs.

Step-by-step

  1. Outcome first. Define the exact artifacts you’ll ship (e.g., prompt v1, QA checklist, change log).
  2. Script the session. Write minute-by-minute timings with role cues and decision points.
  3. Build live. Run timed sprints: facilitator prompts; SMEs critique; scribe logs decisions inline.
  4. Lock ownership. For each artifact, assign owner, due date, and storage path.
  5. Publish recap. Within 24 hours, post minutes, links, and next steps to the KB.

Output contract

Each workshop must produce:

  • Artifact list: prompts, examples, checklists, and risks with owners.
  • Decision log: rationale for choices and items deferred.
  • Follow-up plan: dates, reviewers, and automation flags.

Logging

Save transcript/notes, prompt versions, decision log, and owners to the KB. Tag with workshop ID and link to related projects.

Metrics that matter

  • Artifacts shipped: count delivered within 48 hours.
  • Owner clarity: % of items with named owner and due date.
  • Recap timeliness: recap published within 24 hours.

Troubleshooting
